I grew up in Bucks County, Pennsylvania, a suburb of Philadelphia, where I graduated from 
Neshaminy high school in the top of my class. I then attended the University of Pittsburgh
starting in the Fall of 2019, intending to study physics. In my sophomore year I became 
more interested in pure mathematics and decided to declare a second major in math. 

<img style="float: right;" src="../assets/images/apple_pic.jpeg" width=400px height=auto>

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with Bachelors of Science in Mathematics and Physics and 
decided to attend the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill for a PhD in mathematics,
where I am currently in my first year. While my research interests are not specific yet,
I am most interested in analysis and hope to eventually work with nonlinear PDEs.
